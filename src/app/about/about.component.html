NOIZ Promotions was born under a grey sky in early 2015, and starting as we meant to go on, we held a dozen more events within a year of our first show taking place.

In this time we booked over 100 bands and artists, from local newcomers to international touring acts, and we also booked UK tours for a handful of select acts.

From our flagship all-dayers to the weekday gigs, every one of our events is given the full care and attention it deserves. Our work ethic and lineups have seen NOIZ quickly gaining a reputation as one of the UK's most respected heavy music promoters.
